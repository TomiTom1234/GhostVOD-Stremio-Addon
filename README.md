# 👻 GhostVOD Engine for Stremio/Nuvio

**Combine Multiple IPTV Subscriptions (Xtream Codes / M3U) into One Unified, Clean Stremio Addon!**

If you have one or multiple IPTV subscriptions, you probably hate having to switch to a clunky third-party IPTV player just to watch a movie. Stremio is the ultimate media center, so why not bring your IPTV VODs directly into it, fully cleaned and organized alongside your Debrid links?

GhostVOD is a smart, stateless engine that acts as a bridge between your IPTV providers and Stremio. It completely ignores Live TV clutter and focuses strictly on **VOD (Movies & Series)**.

## 🔥 Key Features
* **True Multi-Provider Support:** Add multiple IPTV subscriptions (Xtream API credentials) into one single Stremio magic link.
* **VOD Only & Zero Catalog Pollution:** No messy Live TV channels or thousands of empty folders. It remains completely invisible until you click on a movie.
* **Smart Hybrid Matching:** Automatically matches titles globally across languages and enforces release years to avoid incorrect duplicates.
* **Transparent Stream Sources:** Each stream explicitely shows the domain it’s coming from, so you know exactly which of your IPTV providers has the best quality/speed.
* **The Perfect Debrid Companion:** It queries your IPTV providers in the background and lists the available streams right next to your Real-Debrid / Torrentio links!
